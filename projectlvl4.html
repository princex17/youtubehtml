AT THE AND WE KNOWING ABOUT MY AREA

Banwala is a small and peaceful village located in the Sirsa district of Haryana, India. The village is known for its hardworking and friendly people. Agriculture is the main occupation, and most of the villagers depend on farming for their livelihood. Wheat, cotton, and mustard are commonly grown crops. Banwala has a calm atmosphere with green fields and clean surroundings. The local culture reflects traditional Haryanvi values. Festivals like Holi, Diwali, and Teej are celebrated with great joy. The village is developing gradually with better roads, education, and facilities. Banwala represents the true spirit of rural Haryana – simple, strong, and united.
